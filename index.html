Research

Research Interests

01

Microelectromechanical Systems

Design and optimization of MEMS devices, including piezoelectric MEMS scanning mirrors and micro-actuation systems using genetic algorithms.

02

Nonlinear Mechanics

Modeling and optimization of beam and shell structures with multiple flexoelectric actuators using sequential quadratic programming and Newton-Raphson iteration.

03

AI for Design

Physics-informed neural networks (PINNs), genetic algorithms, and data-driven methods for structural and actuator optimization.
